Welcome to the Digital Gender and Media Monitoring Tool
This tool is in sections. The first five sections are compulsory, as these provide the basic information on gender gaps in the media. By answering these questions in our monitoring wherever we are we contribute to a global body of knowledge on gender and the media. Sections 6 to 12 are optional. These delve into a greater level of detail on topics, and include images (for newspaper monitoring) and advertisements, which is slightly different to news.

The tool makes use of “skip patterns” i.e. the tool will display (or hide) questions based on previous selections. The navigation bar will give an indication of the different sections of the data capturing tool and the user's current position in the data capturing process. When you finishing capturing data for each story or advertisement submit your input to allow the system to save your entry.

Save and Continue
If you are not able to complete the entry in one go you can "Save and Continue Later". After completing Section 1 (so when on Section 2) on the top, you will see a “Save and Continue later” button. If you want to go check something and return later, click on this and enter your email address twice and save. Whatever you have entered in the application form thus far will be saved and you will receive an email with a link to continue. Kindly note that the email may be sent to your Junk mail box depending on your security settings. You can then click on the link and continue where you left off.

Offline Version
The tool can be used online or offline. If online every response submitted will be sent directly to the online database. If offline, the responses submitted will be saved within your browser and can be synced to the online database when you are back online. This can be done by selecting the “Sync Survey Data” button in the top right corner of the page. The number beside the button indicates the number of responses submitted offline that need to be synced once you are back online. For example, Sync Survey Data (5) indicates you have submitted five responses offline and these need to be synced so that they are uploaded to the online database.
